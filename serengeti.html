The Serengeti features diverse landscapes including endless plains, acacia woodlands, and riverine forests.

ğŸ¦“ The Great Migration

Witness one of nature's greatest spectacles as over 1.5 million wildebeest, 200,000 zebra, and 300,000 gazelle migrate in search of fresh grazing.

  • Timing: Year-round, but varies by season
  • River Crossings: Most dramatic in July-October
  • Calving Season: February-March in southern plains
  • Predators: Lions, cheetahs, and hyenas follow the herds
